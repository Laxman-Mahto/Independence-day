Happy Republic Day!

Celebrate Unity and the Constitution

Why We Celebrate

Republic Day marks the day when the Constitution of India came into effect, making India a sovereign republic. It is a time to honor our democratic values and the leaders who shaped the nation.

Democracy

Republic Day reminds us of the power of democracy and how it defines our nation.

Unity

It is a day to celebrate the unity of a diverse nation and the strength of our Constitution.

Freedom

Republic Day is a reminder of the hard-won freedom that led to the formation of the Republic of India.
